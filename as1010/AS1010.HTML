The main topics of study in Astr 1010 are:
  • The "motions" of the night sky
  • The history of astronomy
  • Gravity and the laws of planetary motion.
  • The planets.
  • Other members of the solar system (excluding the sun!)
Telescopes, the sun, the stars and galaxies are covered in Astr 1020
