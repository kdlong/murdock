The main topics of study in Astr 1020 are:
  • Radiation; telescopes
  • Stellar spectroscopy
  • The Sun
  • The H-R diagram; the Main Sequence lives of stars
  • The deaths of stars; stellar remnants
  • The scale and structure of our own galaxy
  • Other galaxies
