Your Support Is Sweet Music

In January of 1978, eighteen singers from the Cookeville community joined together under the direction of Dr. David Urness to form the choral organization known as Mastersingers. Since that time, literally hundreds of singers have participated, with thousands of audience members enjoying the fruits of their labor.

Mastersingers, which now averages sixty members, continues to enrich the cultural life of the Upper Cumberland area with quality choral presentations which include something for everyone! The group reflects the diversity of the community it serves, both in terms of membership and programming. Robert Shaw, one the greatest conductors of our time said: "A chorus reaches deep into community life, crosses all sorts of social and economic lines, and makes a contribution not only to a city's aesthetics, but also its humanity."

Mastersingers needs your help in order to continue its tradition of providing at least two free concerts each season for the community. We have appreciated your support in the past, and ask that you consider becoming an official patron of the group by joining the "Mastercircle". Donors will be listed in the programs throughout the season. Any contribution you can make will be most appreciated. Thanks for your help!

Craig Zamer, Director
